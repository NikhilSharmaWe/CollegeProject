Fat Loss – What’s the façade?adminFitness About 30% Of The World’s Population Is Obese Today, Which Includes Children And Adults Of All Ages. A Significant Number Of People Are Dealing With Obesity And Are At A Higher Risk Of Developing A Lifestyle Disorder. Obesity Makes A Person Develop An Inferiority Complex And This Leads To Depression And Anxiety. Lately, To Combat All The Above, Everyone Is Driving Themselves Towards Losing Weight. But, Is It Really The Weight One Wants To Lose Or The Fat Or Anything Else? The Food Industry Might Have Complicated The Whole Juggle Between Weight Loss And Fat Loss And We Are Here To Simply Unwind It For You. For A Lay Man, As Long As You See A Graph On The Scale, It Makes You A Happy Puppy! Most Of The Weightwatchers Intend To Fit Back Into Old Clothes, Shrink For A Party Or Size Down Before The Wedding. These Small And Superficial End Results Bring You To Eat Less, Starve More And Follow A Strict Diet Regime Where You Lose Sight Of Good Food. You Might Disagree To This Because Of A Short Term Result You Gained Via These Attempts. But, How Long Will This Sustain? An Average Of 6 People Bounce Back To Old Weight Or Even More In No Time After A Strict Transformation Challenge Or A Narrow Diet Pattern. That’s Majorly Because An Unhealthy Approach Leads You To Detach Yourself From Your Food And Body Functions. This Short Transformation Might Be A Red Signal To Your Old Days.
